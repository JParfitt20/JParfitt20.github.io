About Me

Hi! My name's Joanna and I live just outside of Norwich in the beautiful Norfolk countryside. I grew up in Norfolk and moved back here after graduating from Univeristy.

I graduated from the University of Nottingham in 2014 with a degree in Natural Sciences (Maths and Physics). I then went on to do a PGCE in secondary Mathematics. Since then, I have been teaching maths and music to a wide range of pupils from primary school age up to adults.

I started to learn to code with Treehouse in January 2019, starting with HTML and CSS and moving onto JavaScript and PHP. I enjoy coding because I love to see a project come together. But I also enjoy solving problems and trouble shooting my code.

In my spare time, I love to get creative. I always have at least two knitting or crochet project on the go. And if that isn't enough, I like to make my own clothes from time to time too!
